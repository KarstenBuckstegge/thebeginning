Why do we need thebeginning.info?

Since the Covid-19 pandemic began, many women have been locked down with their abusers.
We want to offer support to anyone in the world who might consider leaving an abusive partner.
But although domestic abuse is a worldwide problem, awareness campaigns and helplines are local.

So we created a new website thebeginning.info where the helpline number for any country in the whole world can be found in a matter of seconds. And no matter where you are, which language you speak or if you want to support a friend living in another country, you can get help.
*All phone numbers are sourced from the official UN WOMEN page  |  LEGAL
